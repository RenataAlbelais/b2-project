About Us

Soap is a product that is used for personal hygiene and to clean certain objects. Our idea is to make a soap based on less harmful products than common soaps. It will be made from aloe Vera, being beneficial for the skin. This idea is to support the third objective of the UN called Health and Well-being.

Aloe Vera is a thorny plant. It originated in eastern and southern Africa. It has been used as a folk remedy for various ailments, burns, and even to improve the appearance of the skin, due to its many observed biological properties.

Why choose Us

Our product is very useful since it can be used by people aged 11 and over, and not only that, it can also be used for various things, the face, the body, washing hands, among others. We made this aloe Vera soap with the intention of reducing the use of products with many chemicals and also increasing personal hygiene since in Mexico only 34% of children and 60% of adults use their hands, and hygiene only It has one objective, to preserve health and prevent disease.
